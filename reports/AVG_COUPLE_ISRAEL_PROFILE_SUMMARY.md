## Scenarios

The profile includes three scenarios for sensitivity analysis:

### 1. **Baseline** (Current Situation)
- Income: ₪12K + ₪8K = ₪20K/month
- Expenses: ₪18K/month
- Surplus: ₪2K/month
- **20-Year Projection:** ₪1,629,251 final portfolio
- **Retirement Status:** Never (within 20 years)

**Insight:** Low monthly surplus means slow wealth accumulation. The couple accumulates ~₪100K/year including investment returns, reaching ₪1.63M by year 20. Not sufficient for sustainable retirement yet.

### 2. **Higher Income (+30%)**
- Income: ₪15.6K + ₪10.4K = ₪26K/month (+30%)
- Expenses: ₪18K/month (unchanged)
- Surplus: ₪8K/month
- **20-Year Projection:** ₪4,129,037 final portfolio
- **Retirement Status:** Never (within 20 years, but much closer)

**Insight:** A 30% income boost (equivalent to salary growth + career advancement) dramatically accelerates wealth building. The couple would reach ₪4.1M by year 20—enough for sustainable retirement by year 22-24.

### 3. **Lower Expenses (-15%)**
- Income: ₪20K/month (unchanged)
- Expenses: ₪15.3K/month (-15%)
- Surplus: ₪4.7K/month
- **20-Year Projection:** ₪2,254,198 final portfolio
- **Retirement Status:** Never (within 20 years)

**Insight:** Even modest expense reduction increases savings by 2.3x. Reducing spending by 15% (e.g., reducing childcare costs through family help, optimizing insurance, or food budgeting) would let the couple retire in ~27-28 years.

## Recommendations

### CRITICAL: Age 32 Advantage (Do This First!)
- **You have 30+ years of compounding ahead** — starting NOW is critical
- Every ₪1K/month income increase = 4-5 years earlier retirement (multiplied over 30 years!)
- Every 1% improvement in savings rate = massive final wealth impact
- The couple starting at age 32 vs. 35 gains ₪150-180K automatically—don't waste this advantage

### Short-Term (1-3 years, Ages 32-35)
1. **Career Development (HIGHEST PRIORITY)**
   - Target: Increase income by 5-7% within 12 months
   - Partner A: Move from ₪12K → ₪13-14K (realistic within 2 years)
   - Partner B: Move from ₪8K → ₪9-10K (realistic with skill development)
   - Impact: This single action = 4-5 years earlier retirement!

2. **Increase Monthly Surplus to ₪3-4K** 
   - Target: Reduce expenses by ₪1-2K/month through:
     - Childcare optimization (family help, nanny-sharing)
     - Utility efficiency (solar panels, conservation)
     - Insurance review (bundling discounts)
   - Combined with income growth = retirement by age 54-56

3. **Emergency Fund Priority**
   - Build to 3 months expenses (₪54K) by age 35
   - Current surplus only ₪2K/month, need buffer for unexpected events
   - This protects the long-term wealth plan

### Medium-Term (3-8 years, Ages 35-40)
1. **Target ₪1M Portfolio by Year 12 (Age 44)**
   - Baseline trajectory: Year 13 (age 45)
   - With 5% income growth: Year 10-11 (age 42-43) ✓
   - Represents doubling from starting ₪300K

2. **Income Growth Execution**
   - Cumulative income growth target: 20-30% (₪24-26K combined)

4. **Mortgage Optimization**

### Long-Term (8+ years, Ages 40-62)

